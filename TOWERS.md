## üèÜ Advanced Strategies

### **Tower Placement Principles**

1. **Damage Maximization**: Place towers where enemies spend the most time
2. **Range Optimization**: Overlap tower ranges for continuous coverage
3. **Economic Efficiency**: Balance cost vs. effectiveness based on wave difficulty
4. **Utility First**: Slow towers increase everyone else's effectiveness
5. **Chokepoint Control**: Focus firepower at natural bottlenecks

### **Resource Management**

- **Early Investment**: Start with Basic + Slow combinations
- **Mid-Game Scaling**: Upgrade to Heavy/Sniper when affordable

- **Emergency Funds**: Always keep $100+ for emergency tower placement
